How to Use:

  1. Navigate to the search page in your YMovies
  2. Enter a search query in the search bar
  3. Click on any filter button next to the search bar
  4. Select your desired filter options from the dropdown
  5. The search results will automatically update with filters applied
  6. Active filters are highlighted and can be removed individually
  7. Use "Clear All" to reset all filters
